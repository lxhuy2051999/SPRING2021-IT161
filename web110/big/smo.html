Social Media Optimization (SMO)

Social media has grown quickly for the past years and becomes a huge space to connect with potential audiences. Up to now, 2021, there are 3.96 billion people, half of the world's population, who currently use social media(Dean How Many People Use Social Media in 2021? (65+ Statistics)). It is essential for websites to have effective social media optimization in order to gain more traffic.

Social media optimization (SMO) refers to the optimization of a website to gain more audiencesâ€™ awareness of that website on social media. SMO is not a must-have factor that a search engine takes account of, but activities and interactions on social media such as likes and shares can improve the rank of a website in SEO effectively.

Similar to SEO, SMO also cares about the quality, not the quantity. Using elements like meta tags, headers, and other semantic tags wisely is essential. To improve the SMO, we should care about components that provide access to social media on our websites. Those components can be links, follow or share buttons. We should place them in positions that can easily attract users' attention and provide better social media consumption. We also need to care about a page when sharing it on social media, we want that page to convey our message to the audiences and show them our prospect. We can search for related hashtags (#) to find the right types of audiences. There are many ways to encourage users to share our website on social media. We can ask them to share their thoughts or treat them when they share our website. people will feel convenience if we integrate social media into our website and allow users to log in with their social media account(Bhardwaj 9 Best SMO Techniques to Boost your Website Traffic).

To sum up, social media optimization is necessary, we should practice social media optimization to gain traffic and popularity for our website.
